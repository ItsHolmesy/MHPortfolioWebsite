How to Play
Welcome to this weird game I built to demonstrate my coding knowledge within javascript. The game goes as follows:

• Goal is to reach 100 points first before your opponent.

• If you roll beneath a 3, you lose all your points for that round and it moves to the next player's turn.

• If you press 'Hold' you keep that rounds points, which are banked into your overall score. And you pass the turn to the next player.

• If you score 12, (Two sixes), your current score doubles. This can be outstanding if you're deep into a round.
